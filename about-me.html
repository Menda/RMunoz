About me

I was born in 1986 in Córdoba (Spain) but 2 years after we moved to a small city called Jaén. I have studied for 6 months at the University of Ústí Nad Labem (Czech Republic) and afterwards I have finished Computer Engineering at Universidad de Jaén.

For a longer while now I have been working at a young start-up named Geomobile where I have been responsible for web developing and mobile applications but I have got to say that web development is definitely my main area of expertise. What is not less significant, I usually manage all GNU/Linux servers that are in use in our Company what clearly augments my substantial and eligible knowledge in this specific field.

What is certainly worth saying, I am a big fan of Free Software, hence I have been cooperating with:

  • KDE: Spanish Localization Team and Raptor.
  • mongoengine: an object-document mapper to connect Python and Mongo DB (website).
  • django-forum: unfortunatelly, not maintained anymore.
  • Technical support: mainly in forums and mailing lists of several distros or frameworks.

Apart from computer technology whatsover I am interested in DJing (mainly electronic and alternative), riding my bike and photography. Nevertheless, my greatest dream is to travel all over this beautiful world to discovered new amazing places and to meet new, outstanding people. This is me.
